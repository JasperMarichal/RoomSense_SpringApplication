About us

Mission

Our mission is to make students and employees more efficient and focused by making their workspaces more comfortable. While improving the overall health and happiness of the workforce.

Vision

Our vision is to boost productivity and health worldwide by maximising the potential of work- and living-environments.

Team introduction

We are a team of five ambitious students. All passionate about new technologies to improve our environment. During our school career we worked on various projects, RoomSense was our biggest project. We are committed to create a device that can help us optimise the usage of classrooms and offices alike. We have the luck to have two powerful shapers in our team. The idea came into their heads and together with the fantastic implementers we implemented the idea into a real product.
